What kind of slides can you create?

Biodiversity
Biodiversity represents the living organisms that live in a certain environment. By choosing to create sides based on biodiversity, you will get slides containing animal information based on the location specified by you.

Land-Use Change
Land-use change represents the change in the environment based on how humans ustilize the resources around them. Over time the landscape around could have changed dramatically. By choosing this, your slides will provide information on how the environment around you has changed in appearance and what might be the cause of these changes.

Climate Change
Climate Change represents the change in global temperature. Over time the world has been heating at an increasing rate, with no real end to it in sight. By choosing this, your slides will discuss information as to what is causing this change, what its been affecting and possible tips on how to slow it down.

Wild Fires
Wild fires represents that change that fires have in the environment. By choosing this, your slides will inform you of the changes fires have created in the environment around you as well as what was the cause of these fires; the slides would also give tips on what can prevent fires from occuring in the wild.

If you are ready to start creating your slides, please push the slides button at the top.
