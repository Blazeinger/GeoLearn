The #FridaysForFuture movement highlights how much young people throughout the world are demanding action in response to the breakdown of our collective environment. We believe that action begins with education. EnviroSlides provides the missing link between environmental scientists and those seeking knowledge.

EnviroSlides is a platform for education about environmental change throughout the world by replacing information found in generic, out-of-date textbooks, with the latest knowledge straight from the scientist’s mouths. The examples provided in our slides are personalised to your location, streamlining important environmental data from large global datasets of biodiversity, climate change and land use change directly to your computer.
